Our Dance School has a Great Mission & Policy

Alma Dance School Frankfurt in Germany has the mission to make everyone dance. Our policy is to provide high-quality dance education accessible to all ages and skill levels, fostering a love for dance in a supportive and inclusive environment. Join us to experience the joy of movement and the benefits of a healthy, active lifestyle.

For our wedding dance we got the opportunity to be trained by Eduardo and Natalia. The challenge was that I had no experience in dancing at all, but my wife has been dancing as a hobby for a few years and has some basic knowledge. The song we chose was "Shallow" by Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper. They created a coreography especially for us and trained us in a total of three dance lessons. With a lot of attention to detail and a lot of patience we were able to learn single steps. It was very helpful that he demonstrated all scenes several times with his partner. After each dance lesson we recorded our progress and practiced at home for a few weeks until the next dance lesson. So I personally managed to get a feeling for the steps and rehearse the whole choreography with my wife. What I thought was not possible was made possible by Eduardo and Natalia, who taught us the wedding dance in a very professional but also fun way, which is why we can highly recommend them.

Stefan & Vicky

Wedding- 23.10.2022
